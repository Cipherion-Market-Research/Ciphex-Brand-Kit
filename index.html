Typography

Three voices, one deliberate system

Geist · digital voice
Capital markets, on-chain.
Body, UI labels, and the sans lead-in of every headline. Display at weight 600.
Merriweather · TradFi voice
the operating layer
Only the headline italic phrase, editorial titles, and large stat figures.
Geist Mono · terminal voice
Preparing access · Q3 2026
Kickers, tags, data labels, dates, meta. Uppercase, tracked, small.
The signature headline
Insights from the Operating Layer
Geist sans lead-in + one Merriweather italic phrase in the accent color. One phrase only — the single most recognizable element of the brand. Fallbacks for vendors: Geist → Helvetica/Arial, Merriweather → Georgia, Geist Mono → SF Mono/Consolas.
